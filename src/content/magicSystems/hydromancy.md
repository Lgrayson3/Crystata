Hydromancy is the manipulation of water in all its states, practiced most extensively in Amphitra, where millennia of deep-water adaptation have produced the most formidable water-based combatants in the civilization.

## Scale of Operations

Hydromancers operate at scales ranging from surgical precision — manipulating water within a single body — to environmental — reshaping coastlines, redirecting currents, weaponizing the ocean itself. The Violet Line blockade doctrine demonstrated Amphitran capacity for total magical warfare at continental scale.

## Aestus

Cloud Sinclair's pre-Oinomantic martial technique, Aestus, is a direct biological application of Hydromantic principles to human bodies — conducting the bloodflow of allied soldiers to optimize their physiological state during combat, regulating adrenaline, managing fatigue, synchronizing reaction times.
