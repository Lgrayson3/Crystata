Oneironautica *(Alak'shud; ah-lack-SHUD; Akkadian alāku "to walk" + šuttu "dream")* — the art of the Dreamwalker. Possessed by approximately one to five percent of the Mavarran population. Crystal Class Mystics.

## Mechanics

Oneironauts can enter the subconscious mind of a target directly through eye contact while the target is awake, moving at just under the speed of a synapse. The practitioner experiences the interior of another person's mind as a vast library — browsable, readable, with individual memories available for examination, relocation, or removal. Capacities can be permanently severed.

## Memory Coins (Shik'piris)

Memories can be extracted and minted into physical objects called memory coins — cloudy grey, slightly luminous, approximately the size and weight of a half-dollar coin. When compressed in the hand, the contained memory is experienced at full sensory fidelity. The market ranges from entertainment and romance to pornography, blackmail material, and intelligence product. Addiction to memory coins — termed "lost in the coins" — is a documented condition.

## The Second Birth

The ceremony performed on children at sixteen, when the Pansilion manifests. Childhood memories are erased and replaced with curated alternatives. Children whose Pansilion does not manifest do not leave the Temple.
