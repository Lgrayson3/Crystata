Hydromnemesis is the practice of listening to the Krenides' recorded memory. The water that has flowed through a place carries the impression of what it touched. The Mnemosynean archivists sit with the Krenides in deep archives and extract the information stored within the current.

## The Archives

Their archives hold a millennium of Sinclair governance recorded in the medium itself. The information belongs to the water, not to the archivists. The institutional doctrine distinguishes between volunteering and responding to inquiry. The archivists will not offer. They may, if properly asked, answer.

## Strategic Significance

Mnemosyne's holdings represent the most comprehensive and tamper-proof record of Thalassan history in existence. The Cult of Sanguine's disposal practices — incinerating remains and sending them off-world through the Clairgate network — bypass the Krenides' memory function entirely. The divine surveillance system never receives the evidence.
