Thanatonautica — the experimental practice of walking beyond the stillness of death into the realms of the dead. Practiced by the Aesthetes in the deep terraces of Tempora over centuries.

## The Channel

This discipline produced the channel through which Raiwata, the Apex Archdevil of Hel, transited from the dead realms into the living. A bridge between worlds that was never meant to carry traffic in both directions. A pocket of literal Hel now exists in the deep, established by Raiwata after his transit.

## What the Aesthetes Have Seen

Some Aesthetes have witnessed the Eighth Circle of Hel with their own eyes. What they found there is not discussed in Tempora. What they brought back — or what followed them back — is a matter the rest of the civilization does not investigate.
