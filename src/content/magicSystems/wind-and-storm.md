Wind & Storm is the martial tradition of Uronoia's Temples of Tempest — the only combat-oriented magic system on Zephyros. Led by Wind Abbots who rise through mastery of Ventus, the Stormcallers train above the clouds on jagged peaks perpetually crowned with storms.

## Techniques

- **Storm-Breath** — channeling gale-force wind through controlled exhalation
- **Sigilstorm Duels** — ritualized combat using wind-sigils traced in the air
- **Storm-Forging** — shaping lightning and wind into temporary constructs

## Philosophy

*"To be is to endure motion. Stillness without choice is death; storm is proof of life."*

The Stormcallers are not soldiers — they are monks preparing for a war they pray never comes. They bond with dragons the way a falconer bonds with a bird of prey. The monks do not welcome tourists.
