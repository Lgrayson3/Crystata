Void Entropy is not learned — it is contracted. Exposure to raw void-energy during interstellar transit permanently alters the practitioner's cellular structure, granting them an innate connection to the entropic forces that govern decay and renewal at the cosmic scale.

## Core Abilities

- **Dissolution**: Breaking molecular bonds in targeted matter, reducing solid objects to constituent particles
- **Reconstitution**: Reassembling dissolved matter into new configurations, though the result is always subtly different from the original
- **Null Field**: Projecting a localized zone where all magical energy is suppressed, rendering lattice-based magic inert

## Costs

Each use of Void Entropy accelerates the practitioner's own entropic decay. Voidborn age at an accelerated rate and must periodically enter stasis to slow their dissolution. The most powerful Voidborn are often the youngest, burning through their remaining lifespan to fuel devastating attacks.
