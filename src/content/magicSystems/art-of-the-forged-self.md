Integrate materials into living flesh until the boundary between body and material dissolves. Stone into skin. Galvaneum into bone. Crystata into nerve. Contemporary name: Transcension.

## Mechanics

This is not cybernetics — a Galvaneum arm has no servos. It has living metal fused to living muscle fused to living nerve, animated by the same vital energy as the rest of the body. The contemporary form is Soma-Weaving: layer-by-layer fusion of Galvaneum into biology at the cellular level.

## The Heraldic Path

The Heraldic Path shapes this forging toward each clan's sacred beast — lupine for the Lykaion, feline for the Felidae, raptor for the Axipitre. A fully transformed Ferridanian does not wear a style. They are one.

## Apex

At the apex, Aurelius Vorn is a seamless fusion of flesh and golden Galvaneum — the distinction between clothing and body has dissolved entirely.
