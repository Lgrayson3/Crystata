The Dance of the Molten Heart is the school of magic practiced in the Freeholds of Rhylos. It is the most physically grounded of the three Pyrtheran schools — geothermal myst channeled through percussive, rhythmic movement.

## Core Arts

- **Magma-Weaving** — directing and shaping flows of molten rock through sustained rhythmic movement
- **Stone-Shaping** — forming architecture and tools from raw stone using the pulse of the volcanic heart
- **Heart-Drumming** — synchronizing group action to the planet's geological pulse, enabling coordinated construction and ritual at massive scale

## Philosophy

The art is relational — fire is not commanded but partnered with. The Dance was developed as a survival necessity before it became a theology. The magma dancers who built the Obsidian Citadels for the Glacian refugees practice this school. Responsible for Pyrthera's architecture — cities that appear grown from the rock rather than constructed.
