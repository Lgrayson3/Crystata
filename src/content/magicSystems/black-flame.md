The Black Flame is the only fire in existence that truly destroys matter and energy. What it touches is gone — not transformed, not reduced, not burned to ash. Gone. Permanently removed from existence.

## The Only Bearer

Carried by one being: Tein-Enhotep, the Last Androsphinx. He breathed it five thousand years ago to annihilate the Androsphinx species, which had been the apex predators of Pyrthera, keeping the human population in the position of prey. He freed a species by ending his own.

He has not used it in millennia. He has not met a foe that warranted it.
