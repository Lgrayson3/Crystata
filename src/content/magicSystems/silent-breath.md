Silent Breath is the magic of the pause. Practiced in the Cloisters of Gran Ornea — bright, silent cathedrals of moonstone and glass perched on skyline castles — this discipline treats breath's true essence not as exhalation but as the stillness between breaths.

## Techniques

- **Breath Occlusion** — the controlled cessation of breath to achieve states of perception beyond ordinary consciousness
- **Silent Communion** — direct psychic contact with Iles through the quality of silence maintained in the Cloisters
- **Thread-Listening** — perceiving and following individual strands of information carried on the Aether

## Philosophy

*"To know is to listen. To speak without pause is to blind oneself to the universe."*

Only Oracles may enter the Cloisters. The restriction isn't secrecy — it's sanctification. One uninitiated voice would shatter the quality of silence required to hear Iles directly.
