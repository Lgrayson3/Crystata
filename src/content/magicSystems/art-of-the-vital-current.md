Move essence from one thing to another. Channel fire into a blade. Charge a crystata with energy. Awaken a construct by directing the Vital Current into its Anima-Script. Contemporary name: Transfusion.

## Infrastructure

This art keeps Ferridane's infrastructure running — the continent-wide Myst-Grid is maintained by Channelers who tend the flow of power through living metal towers. Teleportation, augmentation, construct forges, the Thought Nexus, and the artificial suns that light cities through storms all depend on this art.

## Corruption

In its original form, it was understood as stewardship. Corrupted, it produces soul-batteries: channeling a living soul's essence into a power cell. The Godmind Project's Luxenfallen soul-batteries are the terminal expression of this corruption.
