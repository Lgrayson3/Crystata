The Pansilion *(pan-SIL-ee-on; Akkadian pānu "face" + ṣillu "shadow")* — the Veilborn Mask. Universal. Every Mavarran possesses this ability, which manifests at sixteen during the Second Birth ceremony.

## Mechanics

It permits complete biological reshaping of face, body, bone structure, mass, and voice from dusk until sunrise. Inside Tenebralis, where no sunrise occurs, the Pansilion is permanent. Abroad, the sun strips it involuntarily every morning — an experience consistently described as humiliating and physically unpleasant. The Pansilion is not illusion. It is actual biological transformation.

## Cultural Impact

Inside Tempora, where there is no sunrise to force reversion, a Mavarran can remain shifted for decades without returning to their birth form. The Mavarrans have no native beauty standard rooted in their own appearance. They assemble their beauty from the aesthetic grammar of every other realm they have observed, identifying what made it seductive and wearing it more boldly than the originators dared.
