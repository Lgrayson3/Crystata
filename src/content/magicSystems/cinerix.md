Cinerix, formally known as the Ash-Sight, is the school of magic practiced in the Ashen Vale. Where the Golden Crucible burns away the false and the Molten Heart shapes the new, the Ash-Sight reads what fire has already done.

## Core Arts

- **Ember-Vision** — perceiving the kafer and interior state of another being, reading spiritual truth from the heat-residue of their presence
- **Cinder-Recall** — retrieving the heat of a past moment from its residue, reconstructing events from the ash they left behind
- **Ash-Cloak** — reducing one's spiritual visibility to near-imperceptibility, the opposite of the Pyrtheran norm of radiant presence

## Philosophy

The most subtle of the three Pyrtheran schools. Prolonged practice produces a stillness and detachment that other Pyrtherans find eerie. The Vale's oracles and strategists are the most sought-after in the realm, serving as neutral mediators between Khet and Rhylos.
