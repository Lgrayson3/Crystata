Clairmancy is the art of portal creation, discovered by Juno Sinclair during the founding crisis. It allows the practitioner to open stable gateways between locations — from across a room to across the cosmos. The Clairgate network that connects the nine Realms is the industrial-scale application of this art.

## Combat Applications

As a combat discipline, Clairmancy makes distance irrelevant: a practitioner can open a gate beneath a target's feet and relocate them anywhere, instantly. Cloud Sinclair's demonstrated doctrine — locate the target, open the gate, deliver the resolution directly — renders conventional military geography obsolete.

## Strategic Significance

The art is primarily practiced within the Sinclair bloodline and its institutional affiliates. Control of the Clairgate network gives Thalassor its central position in the inter-realm order. Every realm's relationship to Thalassor is mediated by this dependency. The Sinclairs hold the gates. The gates hold the cosmos together.
