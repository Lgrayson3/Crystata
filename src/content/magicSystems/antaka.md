Antaka is the ability to collapse the distance between a planet and its sun — to force a star out of its orbital position and bring it into contact with a world. It is not a weapon. It is an extinction event. Its existence constitutes nuclear deterrence at planetary scale.

## Holders

Six people hold this power: Sol'Kheret and her five Torchbearers — each personally selected and branded by the Exvisar, each drawn from a different nation to represent the synthesis. A seventh — the Archangel Fiacre — was taught the art by Sol'Kheret during a diplomatic visit, in what was framed as a drunken contest of strength but functioned as a declaration of trust.

## The Glacian Question

Someone called the sun down on Glacia twenty years ago. The Oracles of Zephyros could exonerate Pyrthera and refuse to do so. The actual perpetrator — Raiwata, the Sun Caller, the Apex Archdevil of Hel — remains unnamed by anyone with the authority to be believed.
