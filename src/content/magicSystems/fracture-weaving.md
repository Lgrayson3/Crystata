Fracture Weaving is the simultaneous manipulation of lattice resonance and void entropy. It is an extraordinarily rare — possibly unique — hybrid discipline, with only one known practitioner.

## Core Mechanics

By harmonizing constructive and destructive magical forces, the practitioner can temporarily "fracture" the boundary between structured magic and raw entropy. This creates localized reality distortions that are devastatingly effective in combat but completely unpredictable.

## Costs

Using both lattice resonance and void entropy at once causes severe neurological damage. The discipline demands an extreme physical toll, making sustained use potentially lethal even for its sole practitioner.
