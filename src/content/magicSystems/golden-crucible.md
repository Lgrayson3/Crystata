The Path of the Golden Crucible is the foundational school of magic in the Emberate of Khet. Solar myst is channeled as transformative heat — both physical and metaphysical.

## Core Arts

- **Ignis Judicium (The Golden Flame)** — a metaphysical solvent that burns ego, not flesh. The foundational art of the school and the basis for both the Branding ritual and the Trial of Flame
- **Solar Forging** — imbuing objects with solar myst, creating weapons and artifacts that carry the heat of the seven suns
- **Aegis of the Seven Suns** — projecting shields of solidified sunlight

## Combat Expression

The most common personal combat applications are setting one's fists aflame or breathing fire. Fire-breathing is a difficult and respected technique requiring sustained lung capacity — a true fire-breather is considered extraordinary at home and abroad.

## Philosophy

This school produces the Branding ritual — the practice of inscribing spiritual biography onto the body through sacred burns — and the Trial of Flame, the transformative ordeal through which Pyrtherans prove what they have Become.
