Harmonics of Resonance is the magic of the Crystal Isles — a discipline where sound is both weapon and worship, architecture and prayer. Every practitioner learns that to exist is to vibrate, and to master vibration is to master reality.

## Techniques

- **Voice-Sculpting** — shaping physical matter through precisely pitched vocal frequencies
- **Warding Harmonies** — densely layered wind-barriers that resound with healing energy
- **Choral Invocation** — group-cast magic where multiple voices weave together into effects no individual could produce

## Philosophy

*"To exist is to be heard. To be forgotten is to be unmade."*

Fiacre's own warding magic — densely layered wind-barriers that resound with healing — is harmonic in origin. The Crystal Isles' entire civilization is built on the principle that a perfect harmony can reshape the world.
