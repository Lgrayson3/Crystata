Speak a True Name over a thing, and that thing becomes the Name. Instantly. Completely. This is reality editing: the Mystic's soul overrides what a thing currently is and replaces it. Each major invocation is extremely expensive in myst — one or two per day for even a powerful Mystic.

## Known True Names

- **True Name: Iron** — stone becomes iron. Molecular structure, weight, conductivity — all of it. Foundational to Ferridanian craft
- **True Name: [CLASSIFIED]** — steel comes alive. It thinks. It regenerates. This creates Galvaneum, Living Steel. The Name is a state secret
- **True Name: Static** — spoken over Galvaneum, the life drains out. An off switch for living steel
- **True Name: Hollow** — spoken over a Mystic, their connection to the soul-loom goes dark. They become Nihila, temporarily but completely
- **True Name: Magma** — spoken over someone's shoes. They are standing in lava
- **True Name: Gold** — spoken over anything. Solid gold. Every molecule
