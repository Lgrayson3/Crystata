Oinomancy is wine-based magic, invented by Saint Cloud Sinclair. It uses sacramentally prepared wine as a medium for mystical effects ranging from shared experiential states to binding covenants.

## The Wines

- **The Wine of Shared Dreams** — synchronizes perception across a group, enabling shared tactical awareness
- **The Wine of Shared Horizons** — creates persistent bonds of empathy and mutual understanding
- **The Wine of Shared Fates** — binds outcomes; an oath sworn on it carries enforceable consequences for violation

## The Vintage of the Veil

The oldest and most sacred application of wine-based mysticism. The sacrament that transmits the cumulative memory of every preceding Exvisar into the successor. Through the Concordant Path, the reigning Exvisar carries the experiential weight of a thousand years of governance — not as historical knowledge but as lived memory.
