Lattice Resonance is the foundational magic system of Aethermoor's crystal civilizations. Practitioners undergo Attunement — a ritual bonding of living crystal shards to the nervous system — which grants them the ability to interface directly with the planet's resonant network.

## Core Abilities

- **Memory Reading**: Touching a crystal node reveals stored magical signatures — echoes of spells cast in proximity to the lattice
- **Energy Channeling**: Drawing ambient resonant energy through the lattice to power spells without depleting personal reserves
- **Harmonic Shielding**: Vibrating attuned crystal at defensive frequencies to deflect incoming attacks

## Costs

Prolonged use accelerates crystalline transformation of the practitioner's body. Advanced users progressively lose organic tissue, replaced by living crystal. The final stage — full Crystallization — renders the practitioner immobile but grants them permanent communion with the entire lattice network.
