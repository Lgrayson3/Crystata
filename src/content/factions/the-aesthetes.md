Twenty-five thousand strong, organized, and the only institution in a civilization of individuals. They own the Temple complex, the deep terraces, the Second Birth ceremony, and the Court of Masks proceedings. They are the board. Everyone else is playing on it.

## Function

The Aesthetes maintain the Temple of Ishtar and the rendering chambers where the Pashar'etem — the soul-rendering ceremony — is performed. Through centuries of Thanatonautica practice, they constructed the bridge between the living realms and the dead. They practice Ak'lethem — the Soul-Eating — reaching into living beings and removing what makes them specifically that person while leaving the body operational.

## The Deep

They serve the Etemmu — a cognitive apex predator of incomprehensible scale that exists in the abyss beneath Tempora — because service is the only survivable proximity. The rest of the population believes Vex manages the Etemmu. She does not. She is simply the one mind it has never been able to perceive.

## Rule Two

Do not antagonize the Aesthetes. This is one of three rules in a civilization with no laws.
