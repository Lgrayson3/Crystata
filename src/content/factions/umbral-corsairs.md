The Umbral Corsairs are a fleet-based faction that operates in the vast darkness between Aethermoor and Vorrenth. Unlike the planet-bound Crystal Covenant or the ideologically driven Voidborn Collective, the Corsairs are pragmatists — smugglers, salvagers, and mercenaries who exploit the power vacuums left by the two dominant factions.

## Operations

The Corsairs have no fixed territory. They operate a mobile fleet centered around the Eventide, a massive pre-Sundering vessel of unknown origin. Their primary staging area is the Drift — an uncharted region of space debris and dead satellites orbiting midway between the two known planets, used as a hidden anchorage and black market trading post.

The Drift serves as neutral ground where Crystal Covenant scholars, Voidborn agents, and independent operators can trade in relative safety. Order is maintained through a simple rule: no weapons fire within the Drift's perimeter. Violators are spaced.
