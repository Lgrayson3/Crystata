The Voidborn Collective operates across multiple worlds, united not by geography but by shared exposure to void-energy — the raw entropic force that fills the space between stars. Members are "Touched" during interstellar transit, their biology permanently altered to channel destructive and reconstructive forces.

Unlike the Crystal Covenant's reverence for preservation, the Collective believes that true knowledge comes from dissolution. They seek to unmake the Crystata and harvest the raw energy stored within, using it to fuel a new epoch of creation.
