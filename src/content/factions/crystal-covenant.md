The Crystal Covenant traces its lineage back to the Architects — the pre-Sundering civilization that originally wove Aethermoor's crystal lattice. Their order is devoted to reconstructing the broken network node by node, believing that a fully restored Crystata would grant access to the accumulated magical knowledge of every civilization that has ever inhabited the planet.

Members undergo a ritual called Attunement, bonding a shard of living crystal to their nervous system. This grants them the ability to read residual energy signatures in the lattice but slowly transforms their flesh into translucent crystal over decades.
