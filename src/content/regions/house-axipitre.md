The Northern Highlands. Cliff-face cities connected by soaring bridges, deep mines, resonating crystata veins. The cosmos is a web of flowing energies to be understood and guided. Construction as a mode of being. Their sacred form is engineered, not grown.

### Sacred Art

Transfusion (the Art of the Vital Current) as primary discipline. Channeling essence between objects, enchanting weapons, awakening constructs.

### Architecture

Cities built into cliff-faces. Soaring workshops in jagged mountain ranges. Where the crystata veins pulse and the resonating mines hum.

### Heraldic Form

Dark bronze, avian augmentation — constructed, not grown. Wings crafted and bonded. Living hieroglyphs. Beauty as function.

### The Godmind Project

The clan that produces both the Realm's greatest wonders and its greatest heresies. The same mind that crafts a perfect bronze wing crafts a god. Crex came from here. The Godmind Project — the attempt to engineer a synthetic deity using enslaved souls — is an Axipitre operation hidden from the Exvisar.

### Atmosphere

*The clan's sin is amorality. Any source of power can be channeled if efficient.*
