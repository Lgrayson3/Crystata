West of Auraeas across a great sea. A separate continent with its own governance, sharing the overarching civilizational framework under Iles. The relationship is like Germany and Switzerland under the Holy Roman Empire — distinct nations, shared faith. Maias's identity is custodial: the continent that holds the oldest living knowledge in the world.

### The Grand Garden

The oldest, deepest, most knowledge-dense repository on the planet. Vast, luminous garden-temples where every bed, every vine, every ancient tree holds a different branch of understanding. The air shimmers with accumulated knowledge — denser, richer, almost visible. Walking into the Grand Garden of Meliadas feels like walking into a living mind. The knowledge isn't shelved. It's blooming.

### The Templar

Near Meliadas: a genuinely good school in a genuinely peaceful world. Students study agriculture, rhetoric, history, philosophy, the harmonic arts, the science of tending knowledge gardens. The campus where Alciel arrives. The school where the burning question — *why was Aracice exiled?* — has been the defining debate topic for twenty years.

### Atmosphere

*Where the cosmos comes to learn, in gardens that have been breathing for centuries, on a continent that measures its worth not in power but in what it has kept alive.*
