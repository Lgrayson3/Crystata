Unlike the fractured terrain of the Reach, Luminarch Basin preserves a near-complete section of Aethermoor's original crystal lattice. The resonant network channels ambient starlight into a warm, steady glow that sustains lush bioluminescent forests and crystalline lakes.

The Crystal Covenant maintains its seat of power here, drawing on the intact lattice to fuel their deepest rituals.
