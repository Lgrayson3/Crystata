The ungoverned territories between clan holdings. Tens of millions of inhabitants. Sovereignty. You are your own authority. You answer to your own competence. One rule: do not start what you cannot finish.

### Sacred Art

All three arts, practiced in corrupted, improvised, and unrestricted forms. Black-market augmentation without covenant. The arts spread here because they work, regardless of whether the practitioner understands the theology.

### Architecture

Functional, improvised, built by craftsmen who could have been guild-masters and decided the guild was too slow.

### Heraldic Form

No standardized form. Every body is a personal statement. Unrestricted augmentation designed by engineers who got tired of being told what they could not build.

### Atmosphere

*Not where Ferridane breaks down. Where Ferridane runs unregulated. The weapons end up in the wrong hands. The Outlands craftsman did not do any of that. He just built and sold. What happens next is not his problem.*
