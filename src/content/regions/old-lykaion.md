The Southern Marches and Fractured Marches. The ontology of the Will. True power is earned through refinement of the self. The body is the raw material. Strength justifies possession. The mountain does not ask permission to be the mountain.

### Sacred Art

Transcension (the Art of the Forged Self) as primary discipline. Stone infused directly into skin. They worship Brokkae over Edgerinne.

### Architecture

Fortified port-citadels, massive industrial forges, volcanic ranges. Built for endurance, not display.

### Heraldic Form

Stone-gray, massive silhouette. Broad shoulders, heavy jaw. Temple guardians carved from mountainside. The brow ridge deepens, the musculature thickens into something that could crack a city gate.

### Atmosphere

*They are raiders. They are also the keepers of the old names. When a Lykaion smith speaks a True Name over iron at a forge heated by the strongest hands in the world, that is the Art practiced with something close to its original reverence.*
