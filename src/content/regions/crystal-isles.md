A scattered archipelago of floating islands drifting across the Zephyrosian seas. Crystalline, semi-transparent, built entirely for acoustics — every plaza doubles as a stage. Bridges of glass connecting isles. Windchime spires ringing perpetually with music that is simultaneously architecture and prayer. The light through these structures creates prismatic effects that paint the air itself with color. The Isles look like they're singing, even from a distance.

### Governance

Choral Councils where representatives sing arguments and votes are cast as layered chords. The most harmonious resolution becomes law. Families arranged into choirs. Children learn harmony before arithmetic. Lies are inaudible — the Isles reject deceit like a discordant note.

### Vulnerability

The most vulnerable point in all of Zephyros. A culture of consensus means a lie wrapped in beauty can pass unnoticed. A perfect chorus doesn't prove truth — it proves unity of sound. A single corrupted Isle can hum in dissonance for years before anyone notices the songs sound strange.

### Atmosphere

*Floating crystal and perpetual song, where beauty is worship and the most dangerous thing is a harmony so perfect it hides a lie.*
