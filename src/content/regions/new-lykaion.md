The transitional territories between the Southern Marches and the Central Plains. Edgerinne's teaching read as Darwinism. Natural selection. Survival as proof of fitness. Pressure as filter rather than covenant. They want what the Felidae have and believe they are evolutionarily entitled to it.

### Sacred Art

All three arts, with biological ambition as the organizing principle. Soma-Weaving practiced as devotional art.

### Heraldic Form

Gilt-bronze, angular, built for pursuit rather than collision. Lean jackal silhouette. Galvaneum traces lean muscle like filigree. Something funerary about them — performing sacred embalming on themselves while still alive.

### Atmosphere

*The most politically dangerous faction in Ferridane.*
