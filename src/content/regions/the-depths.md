The Aesthetes operate here. The rendering chambers are in the deep. Things come up from the depths.

### Environment

The abyss. Where the distinction between city and wild ceases to be meaningful. The twenty-five thousand Aesthetes maintain the Temple of Ishtar, the rendering chambers where the Pashar'etem is performed, and — through centuries of Thanatonautica practice — the bridge they constructed between the living realms and the dead. A pocket of literal Hel exists in the deep, established by Raiwata after his transit through the channel the Aesthetes built.

### The Etemmu

The Etemmu — a cognitive apex predator of incomprehensible scale — exists in the deep. The Aesthetes serve it because service is the only survivable proximity. The rest of the population believes Vex manages the Etemmu. She does not. She is simply the one mind it has never been able to perceive.

### Atmosphere

*No imported light. No crystata. The only illumination rises from below, and what rises from below is not bioluminescence.*
