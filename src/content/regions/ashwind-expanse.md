The Ashwind Expanse dominates Vorrenth's equatorial zone. Temperatures here can melt exposed metal during the twin-sun zenith, forcing all surface travel into the brief twilight hours. Beneath the dunes, precursor ruins extend for kilometers, their corridors sealed by crystalline locks that respond to no known magic system.
