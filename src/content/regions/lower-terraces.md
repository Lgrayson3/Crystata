Population drops away. Violet glow dominant. Sound is the cascading water and nothing else.

### Environment

No Luxanian crystata. No Ferridanian fire. The warm light sources that make the upper terraces habitable are absent. What remains is native bioluminescence: cold blue water, deep violet flowers. The city becomes monochrome. Navigation depends on familiarity, not sight.

### Function

The transition zone. The boundary between inhabited city and wild is indeterminate here. The Luxenfallen perimeter watch — composed entirely of enslaved empaths reprogrammed to a single behavioral directive — occupies this space. The La'Sumu, nameless things faster than eye-tracking, probe the perimeter from below and above.

### Atmosphere

*Monochrome blue-violet. Near-total fog. Bioluminescent Luxenfallen bodies providing ambient light — the conquered realm's divine heritage illuminating the transition zone of the civilization that destroyed it.*
