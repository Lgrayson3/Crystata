Two distinct sapient races sharing a single subaquatic society that predates every humanoid settlement on the planet. Sea-Sirens present humanoid above the waist and aquatic below — passive, immobile, singing from coastal rocks. What lies beneath the waves functions as a one-way door. Sky-Sirens present as bird body with woman's head — active, airborne, not harpies, and will respond to the comparison with immediate violence.

### Primary Grievance

Stop doing war on their roof. Every naval engagement, every military exercise, every piracy interdiction happens on the ceiling of their civilization. The surface world has treated Siren territory as open water for a millennium without once acknowledging that another civilization lives under it.

### Alliances

Amphitra maintains the oldest formal defense alliance — generations of coexistence, mutual benefit, amphibious training, deep-water magical knowledge. Cloud Sinclair maintains an independent channel through Lupe Volantis, bypassing Amphitran primacy — a strategic intelligence asset and a diplomatic powder keg if discovered.

### Atmosphere

*The reminder that the surface world is a guest on a planet it does not own, and the landlord has been patient, but patience is not the same as permission.*
