The lowlands where the River of Flames spreads into the Ignition Rift. Canyons and plateaus split by rivers of magma, volcanoes in sustained gentle eruption. The air shimmers with heat haze and carries the low rumble of the earth. Settlements built on massive basalt arches spanning the lava flows, or carved into cliff faces. The architecture is organic — cities that appear grown from the rock rather than constructed, shaped by the Dance of the Molten Heart. The Obsidian Citadels built for the Glacian refugees are here: cold inside, designed for someone else's comfort.

### Life

Pastoral. Rhylians herd volcanic-adapted megafauna that follow magma flows the way terrestrial animals follow water. The herder reads the flows and guides the herd through safe corridors between active eruptions. This is mutualism, not domestication — if the herder reads the flow wrong, the magma does not wait and the animals do not care. Rhylians are also the realm's master architects and stonemasons.

### Visual Details

Denser firesilk draping than Khet, in earthtone golds and volcanic reds. Builder's tools alongside weapons. Bodies shaped by stonework. Architecture spanning rivers of glowing magma. The Obsidian Citadels — given freely, cold inside, asked for nothing.
