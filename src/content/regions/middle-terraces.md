Where children are kept. Where the fog thickens. Where the stone reveals its raw nature.

### Environment

Quieter. Fog thicker. Residences carved deeper into the rock face, away from social visibility. Less maintained surfaces reveal the stone's raw character — gold veining more prominent where the polish gives way. The Temple of Wizaer, site of the Second Birth ceremony, occupies this tier. Oneironaut cloisters — described as abyssal libraries where the waking world and the Dreamscape blur — are hidden here.

### Function

Where the majority of the population actually resides. Where children spend sixteen unmasked years behind walls — the only zone where real faces exist in the waking world. During daylight hours when the Pansilion is biologically inactive, parents revert to their birth form in private. The domestic life of Tenebralis happens here, out of sight.

### Atmosphere

*Cooler-toned. Bioluminescent water glow dominant, less crystata warmth. Narrower passages. Architecture more organic, more cave-like. Belladonna presses closer where maintenance is less frequent.*
