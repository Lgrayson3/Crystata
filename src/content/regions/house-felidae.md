The Central Plains. Capital: Steelheart. Synthesis and perfection. Apex predators in the ecological sense — they consume what the other clans produce and integrate it into governance. The Felidae see themselves as possessing the divine right to reshape the cosmos.

### Sacred Art

All three arts practiced in concert, framed through self-perfection and sovereign will. They are dependent on the other clans' output and will never admit it.

### Architecture

Geometric, breathtaking, cities as living demonstrations of Transmutation — stone that obeys command. Steelheart's architecture makes Thalassor's Salacica look provincial.

### Heraldic Form

Golden lions. Polished brass, feline augmentation. Poised, sovereign, composed until the instant they are not.

### Atmosphere

*When someone in Thalassor or Pyrthera thinks "Ferridane," they think Felidae. That monopoly on representation is itself a source of war.*
