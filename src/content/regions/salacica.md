The capital continent and seat of the Sinclair dynasty, the Symposium, and the Clairgate nexus. Salacica projects power across the planet through the Clairgate network, the military apparatus, and the Temple's institutional reach.

### Governing Philosophy

Abundance as institutional mandate. The Sinclairs hold the Spring, the Temple holds the doctrine, and the Symposium provides the diplomatic infrastructure where every realm's business is conducted over wine.

### Sacred Art

Oinomancy — the art of wine-based magic, invented by Saint Cloud Sinclair. Clairmancy — the art of portal creation, discovered by Juno Sinclair and the foundational technology of the inter-realm order. Both are practiced primarily within the Sinclair bloodline and its affiliated institutions.

### Architecture & Environment

The capital city descends from the Spire — the Sinclair palace in near-transparent Crystata — through the luminous Heights, the terraced marble districts, to the stone waterfront. Krenides flow through open channels in every district. Shrines line every waterway. The Temple of Salacia is more beautiful than the palace, because the dynasty would never visually outshine the goddess it claims to serve. The Synedrion occupies a structure resembling a cathedral functioning as a parliament.

### Key Visual Details

Crystata refracting Krenides light into prisms. Blue thread woven into clothing hems as devotional practice. Water-drop motifs on doorframes. Patron sigils on workshop doors. The visual gradient is the social gradient — translucent at the top, warm in the middle, functional at the base.

### Atmosphere

*A living city where every person is simultaneously genuine and performing, and the performance is the genuine article.*
