The brightest, most populated, most curated tier. This is what visitors see. This is what Tenebralis exports as its image.

### Environment

Maximum concentration of Luxanian crystata produces warm gold luminescence against the reflective onyx. The combined chromatic palette at any given moment: cold blue from water, deep violet from belladonna, warm gold from stolen Luxanian light, flickering amber-orange from Ferridanian fire crystata — all on polished black stone with gold veining that shifts depending on which light source dominates. Fog is thinnest here; visibility approaches its maximum of ten feet.

### Function

The symposiums, Veiled Houses, performance-front commerce, bars, social clubs, theaters, and restaurants. The Curators of the Court of Masks operate from this tier. The Pansirru — a falcon-like predator whose mirrored feathers broadcast the secrets of its targets — forces symposiums to relocate when sighted. Glass-winged Sarrith insects congregate on uncovered mirrors, feeding on reflected insecurities.

### Atmosphere

*Warm-toned. Dense. Reflective. Aposematic fashion in saturated colors against polished black. Crowds of assembled impossible beauty — because every face was designed tonight. Constant ambient sound: falling water, music, the hum of performance.*
