The Shattered Reach stretches across Aethermoor's northern hemisphere — a labyrinth of canyons carved by ancient cataclysm. Jagged crystal formations rise hundreds of meters from the canyon floors, each humming with residual energy from the Sundering, the catastrophic event that fractured the planet's original lattice network.

Travelers report hearing whispers in the harmonic resonance of the crystals, fragments of spells cast centuries ago still echoing through the lattice.
