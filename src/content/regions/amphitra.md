Amphitra predates the Sinclair dynasty and claims to predate every humanoid settlement on the planet. The alliance with Salacica is honored because it serves Amphitran interests. The blockade doctrine — the capacity for total magical warfare demonstrated by the Violet Line — remains foundational to Amphitran strategic identity.

### Governing Philosophy

Strength remembered. The alliance with Salacica is honored because it serves Amphitran interests. Amphitra holds more deliberative sway in the Synedrion than observers assume.

### Sacred Art

Hydromancy — the manipulation of water in all its states, practiced with a fluency that reflects millennia of deep-water adaptation. Amphitran Hydromancers are the most formidable water-based combatants in the civilization, and their capabilities extend to environmental manipulation at scales sufficient to reshape coastlines.

### Architecture & Environment

Half-submerged cliff cities built into the water rather than on top of it. Streets become channels. Buildings descend below the waterline into functional submerged chambers. No clear boundary between terrestrial and aquatic space. The architecture originated in the sea and extended upward.

### Key Visual Details

Bioluminescence — veins tracing blue-white patterns beneath dark olive skin, shifting with emotional state. Gargantuan serpentine dragons. Sky-leviathan fleets. Boneships. Deep Rider Ligeia at the head of the most martially formidable navy in the civilization. Amphitrans glow when they feel — physiologically incapable of concealing emotion.

### Atmosphere

*Standing inside the ocean's memory of itself — ancient, luminous in the dark, and quietly certain that everything built on the surface is temporary.*
