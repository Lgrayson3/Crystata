Captain Rhenn Ashfall is the leader of the Umbral Corsairs and a former operative of the Voidborn Collective. She deserted the Collective after surviving a catastrophic void-exposure event that should have killed her. The event left her with a unique hybrid ability — she can channel both lattice resonance and void entropy simultaneously, a discipline she calls Fracture Weaving.

## Reputation

Rhenn is a tactical genius with a reputation for impossible heists. She once stole an entire lattice node from the Shattered Reach, smuggling it aboard her flagship, the Eventide, and selling it to Voidborn researchers on Vorrenth.

## The Eventide

Her flagship, the Eventide, is a massive pre-Sundering vessel of unknown origin. It serves as the mobile headquarters of the Umbral Corsairs and the centerpiece of their fleet operations in the Drift.
