Xal-Uron lives among the stormclouds above Uronoia — black, streaked with gold, serpentine, blotting out the visible sky. He is one of two Gonlan that anchor the divine infrastructure of Zephyros.

Above the peaks and below the stratosphere, Xal-Uron serves as a living conduit. The Aetheric particles shed by Aeralithe cascade down through his form, transforming into the golden lightning — the Fulguror — that keeps the Realm just. He is the storm that the Stormcallers train beneath, the darkness that makes Uronoia the only place on Zephyros with dramatic chiaroscuro.
