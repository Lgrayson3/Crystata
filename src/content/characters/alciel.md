Alciel is the Dragonson — son of Aracice the Star-Eater, the Dragon-God whose murder set the cosmos into its current crisis. He arrives at the Templar near Meliadas in Zephyros, where the burning question — *why was Aracice exiled?* — has been the defining debate topic for twenty years.

Trained by Tein-Enhotep in the discipline of the martial, Alciel carries a burden no one fully understands: the truth of the cosmos is branded on his godfather's spine in a script only the gods can read, and the only translator — Nyx, his grandmother — was sealed away specifically to prevent her from reading it to him. The Last Androsphinx mentoring the Last God. Both carrying the weight of being the final member of a category that used to have more names in it.
