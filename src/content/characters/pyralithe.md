Pyralithe is the Gonlan of the Upper Sky — one of two primordial draconic beings residing in Pyrthera, children of Aracice the Star-Eater. Pyralithe represents the transcendent solar flame above: the fire that falls from the sky, the suns' gift made manifest in draconic form.

At the start of the series, both Pyralithe and Agnivar are in mourning — children of a murdered god, feeling the sickness from Hel spread through worlds their father built.
