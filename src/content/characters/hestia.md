Hestia is a being made entirely of fire. She possesses every flame that has ever existed, including the one she created: the Golden Flame, a fire that dissolves the ego — the constructed self, the sustained false narrative, the lie a person maintains about who they are. In a cosmos where the most dangerous enemy operates through the systematic manipulation of identity, Hestia's creation is the natural predator of the entire Mavarran civilization.

## The Golden Flame

The Golden Flame does not search or discriminate. It reaches for what is false. If truth exists underneath, the person survives, healed. If nothing exists underneath — if the subject is hollow or clings to the false self past the point of severance — the Flame continues through the mind and everything it is composed of, reducing the whole to ash. This outcome is called Sarcax.

## The Goddess of the Hearth

To imagine Hestia, imagine Sol'Kheret ten times larger and composed of fire itself. She is the goddess of the hearth — the fire at the center of the home, the one the family gathers around — and she is as terrifying as she is warm. The two qualities are not in tension. She is a mother who loves her children completely and will let them fail, because failure is how one learns.

## Solar Synthesis

Her blessing to the Pyrtheran people is Solar Synthesis: the metabolic ability to convert stellar radiation into vitality, myst, and emotional resilience. Under seven suns, it renders them functionally inexhaustible.
