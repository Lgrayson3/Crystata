Aeralithe dwells above the stratosphere of Zephyros — above even Xal-Uron, above the stormclouds, at the boundary where atmosphere becomes cosmos. The child of Aracice and Iles, Aeralithe sheds the Aetheric particles that define the Realm: the faint yellow-green myst that drifts down from the stratosphere like luminous pollen.

These particles cascade through Xal-Uron, transforming into Fulguror — the golden lightning of divine justice. The scales Aeralithe sheds are the source of the yellow-green luminescence that manifests in the eyes and veins of every Zephyrosian, the visible mark of breathing a Goddess's air.
