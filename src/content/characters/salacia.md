Salacia is the Goddess of Water. Her domain is water, healing, and abundance. Her blessing is the Krenides — sacred waters originating from the First Spring in Salacica, flowing through engineered channels in every city on every continent of the planet. The Krenides heal wounds, cure illness, and slow aging in those with sustained exposure. They are available to everyone — Mystic and Nihila, aristocrat and dockworker, native and visitor. The healing is immediate, unconditional, and empirically verifiable.

## The Covenant

Salacia's covenant is with Thalassor specifically. Her provision is for her people — the approximately three hundred million who live under her care on her world. The covenant does not extend to other realms. The Krenides are exported abroad as commerce, not as obligation. Salacia has no jurisdiction over crystata, no interest in wealth distribution, no mechanism for enforcing economic behavior. She provides the water. What mortals build on top of that provision is their own responsibility.

## The Theological Relationship

The theological relationship is not a matter of faith. It is empirically confirmed every morning. The water heals. The goddess is real. The population's compliance with the theocratic order originates in this daily confirmation. The religion holds because it is demonstrably true.

Salacia's patience is not infinite. Her silence on the Bloodless, on the Mavarran infiltration, on the slow corruption of the covenant she established — this silence may not indicate approval. It may indicate a threshold that has not yet been crossed. The goddess who heals can also drown. She has done it before. The water remembers.
