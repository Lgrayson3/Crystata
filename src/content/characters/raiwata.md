Raiwata is the Sun Caller, the Apex Archdevil of Hel. He transited from Hel into the living realms through the channel constructed by the Aesthetes' centuries of Thanatonautica practice — a bridge between the living realms and the dead that was never meant to carry traffic in both directions.

He called the sun down on Glacia twenty years ago, an act of Antaka that destroyed an entire realm. The Oracles of Zephyros could exonerate Pyrthera — whom the cosmos suspects — and refuse to do so. Raiwata remains unnamed by anyone with the authority to be believed, while Sol'Kheret carries the accusation of an atrocity committed by the thing she should be hunting.

A pocket of literal Hel exists in the deep terraces of Tempora, established by Raiwata after his transit.
