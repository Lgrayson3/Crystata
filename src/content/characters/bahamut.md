Ferridane does not have a natural Gonlan — a primordial dragon-guardian born of the Dragon-God Aracice. What Ferridane has is a manufactured one.

The Diamond Gonlan Bahamut is the product of the Godmind Project: a black-budget Axipitre operation to engineer a synthetic deity. A vessel of pure diamond crystata, constructed using the three Sacred Arts pushed beyond their sacred limits, powered by Luxenfallen soul-batteries — crystallized essences of tormented, enslaved people. The stolen God-Eyes of Crex Falcon Axipitre were installed in its skull to grant it divine discernment.

## The Trap

The project was hidden from Aurelius Vorn himself, because even in Ferridane, building a false god is heresy in a cosmos where the Pantheon is real, present, and has direct emissaries walking among the population.

Bahamut is the terminal expression of the Axipitre sin: the belief that any result can be engineered if the engineering is good enough. It is also a Mavarran trap. The daughters of Mavarra provided the soul-batteries and the crystata from the Lightwells. They asked only for a portion of the fruit this god would bear when it awakened. Failure means spiritual and material bankruptcy for the Axipitre. Success means breaching the divine realm of Dystan, inviting retribution from the Pantheon, and opening a path for Raiwata to flood through the breach.

Ferridane's greatest creation is an engine of its own potential destruction, built by the smartest people on the continent, funded by the most evil people in the cosmos, and hidden from the one man whose authority might have stopped it.
