Syrel Vaine's body is more crystal than flesh — her skin a translucent lattice through which faint light pulses in rhythm with Aethermoor's resonant network. She can read the memories embedded in crystal nodes with a touch, experiencing centuries of stored magical knowledge in moments.

Her leadership of the Covenant is unchallenged but controversial. Some whisper that her extreme Attunement has eroded her humanity, that she now thinks in the slow, geological timescales of the crystal itself rather than in the urgent tempo of mortal threat.
