Fiacre is an Archangel of Zephyros whose warding magic — densely layered wind-barriers that resound with healing — is harmonic in origin, drawn from the Crystal Isles' tradition of Resonant Song.

Sol'Kheret taught Fiacre the art of Antaka during a diplomatic visit, in what was framed as a drunken contest of strength but functioned as a declaration of trust. This makes Fiacre the seventh holder of the power to collapse the distance between a planet and its sun — and the only non-Pyrtheran to possess it.
