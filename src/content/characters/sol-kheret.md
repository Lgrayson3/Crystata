Sol'Kheret is the most beloved Exvisar in Pyrtheran history. Her titles include Warden of the Seven Suns, Torchmother of Becoming, the Ember Queen, and Invictar Solis — the Unconquered of the Sun. She is a Shaman Queen: a sovereign who governs her civilization and a shaman who channels the goddess Hestia through her own body. She sees the kafer — the visible spiritual aura — of those before her, perceives the interior state of hearts and egos, and administers the Golden Flame as a means of mystical healing through spiritual intervention.

## The Branding

She is a former Templar who underwent the Branding of the Golden Flame — the full application, the one that leaves most recipients mindless. It hardly changed her. The Flame went through and found so little falsehood that she emerged almost exactly as she entered. Then Hestia descended and accepted the formalized ritual duel that determines the Exvisarship: fourteen days of the full Sacred Braid — fighting, dancing, lovemaking — with the goddess, while maintaining every torch in the court lit. She maintained every torch for the duration.

## Appearance

Dark-skinned, statuesque, hair braided with sunfire and gemstones, seven pupils in her eyes. Her body is the most extensively branded in the realm. In her Court of Becoming — the roofless obsidian amphitheater — she sits on the floor because the floor is already hers. When she raises a hand, the drums stop. When the drums stop, the world stops with them.

## The Thousand-Year War

She has been at war with Tenebralis for the duration of her reign. She is the only member of the Exvisarelim with her sights set on the Realm of Shadows — not as a political adversary but as a spiritual sickness she can see with the clarity the Golden Flame grants her. She is the cosmos's most correct diagnostician and its most convenient suspect.
