Aurelius Vorn is Edgerinne's direct servant and the paragon of House Felidae. He governs from Steelheart, the capital city of the Central Plains, and answers to the goddess without intermediary. He is the face of Ferridane to the nine Realms — the man foreign dignitaries meet, the authority other Exvisars negotiate with.

## The Forged Self

In full authority, Aurelius Vorn is more integrated material than original biology. Galvaneum — living, thinking steel — has been fused into his body at the cellular level through the Art of the Forged Self until the question of where the man ends and the material begins is unanswerable. He does not appear armored. He appears *inevitable* — a seamless fusion of flesh and golden living metal, the Felidae heraldic form realized at its absolute apex. The lion does not wear armor because the lion does not need to.

## The Hero's Journey

Whether Aurelius was forged by genuine sacred process or built by the best Axipitre engineers on the continent is a question the civilization does not ask aloud. His authority is real. His power is real. The Hero's Journey — a coast-to-Steelheart footrace across the Pangaea — ends with the winner landing a single blow on Aurelius Vorn. Nobody defeats Aurelius.

## Governance

His inability to control the Lykaion clans abroad is a known vulnerability that other Exvisars exploit diplomatically. The Godmind Project was hidden from him because even in Ferridane, building a false god is heresy.
