Hydralithe is a moon-sized hydra — one of Aracice's Gonlan children, the category of divine constructs that exist at the intersection of god and weapon. In ancient times, before the Sinclair dynasty, before the Synedrion, before the founding history that every Thalassan child learns, Hydralithe drowned the world. The waters rose and covered the planet and washed away what Salacia could no longer tolerate.

This is not myth. This is recorded in the Krenides. The water remembers it because the water did it.

## The Precedent

The precedent exists within the divine architecture for catastrophic correction on a planetary scale. The civilization built on water lives at the pleasure of the water. The goddess who heals can also drown. She has done it before. The water remembers. Hydralithe still exists. The moon still watches.
