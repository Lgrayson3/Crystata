Kael Duskwarden has patrolled the Shattered Reach for over two decades, his body bearing the telltale crystalline scarring of deep Attunement. His left arm is almost entirely translucent crystal — a consequence of channeling too much lattice energy during the Siege of Cairn Hollow.

He is fiercely loyal to the Crystal Covenant but harbors doubts about their leadership's willingness to engage the Voidborn threat directly. Where the Covenant prefers ritual and patience, Kael believes in steel and decisive action.
