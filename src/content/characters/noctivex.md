Noctivex is the Primal Gonlan of the Realm of Shadows. Eyeless. Many-winged. Coiled in shadow. Its scales are vantablack — a surface that absorbs all light and reflects none, rendering its body a moving void in any visual field.

It drinks minds: proximity to Noctivex produces progressive cognitive dissolution, not through any active predatory behavior, but as a passive effect of existing near something operating at a scale the mortal nervous system was not built to process. It eclipses all light within its radius.

It is Alciel Aquilon Karai's maternal inheritance made flesh — the form drawn from his mother Nyx's domain, the Goddess of Night sealed behind the Veil of the Life-Blind.
