Erebus is the God of Darkness. His domain encompasses all forms of darkness — physical, spiritual, and cognitive. His gift to the realm is the Shade: a dampening field that obscures Tenebralis from the divine surveillance maintained by other gods. Within the Shade, actions that would otherwise trigger divine attention or intervention remain unobserved.

Erebus does not govern his people. He provides the conditions under which they govern themselves — or, more precisely, the conditions under which they are free to do as they choose without divine consequence. He murdered Aether, God of Light, enabling the conquest of Luxania. His relationship with Vex Mavarra is one of mutual convenience: she functions as the civilization's theology incarnate, which allows him to remain even deeper in shadow.
