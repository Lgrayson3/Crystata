Aeon is the Exvisar of Zephyros — a being whose very presence shapes the rhythm of the Realm. His rare utterances carry weight that reshapes weather patterns and social currents alike. His silences are equally meaningful, setting the tempo by which the entire civilization breathes.

Where other Realms have wardens who act, Aeon is a warden who *listens*. His authority is not imposed but felt — a gravitational force in the social fabric of Zephyros that bends all things gently toward equilibrium.
