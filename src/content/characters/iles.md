name: Iles
species: Goddess
title: The Breath of All That Lives
description: Goddess of Air, Language, and Comprehension — patron deity of Zephyros, who is not merely worshipped but is the very medium her people exist within.
homeworld: zephyros
---

Iles is the air itself. She is not a distant deity enthroned above her world — she is the medium through which every word is spoken, every breath drawn, every thought carried on the wind. Her presence is felt in the Aether that shimmers near gardens and sacred sites, in the warmth behind a Mystic's eyes, in the way a flower leans toward a speaking scholar.

Her enforcement is Fulguror — golden lightning that answers the moment violent intent crystallizes. She does not punish after the fact. She prevents. The gentleness of Zephyros is not temperamental. It is architectural. And she is the architect.

You can almost hear her clearly if you're still enough — but even if you're not, she is felt.
