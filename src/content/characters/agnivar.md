Agnivar is the Gonlan of the Volcanic Heart — one of two primordial draconic beings residing in Pyrthera, children of Aracice the Star-Eater. Composed of lava bones and dragon glass, Agnivar is a being of the deep earth, representing the fire that rises from below.

Between Pyralithe above and Agnivar below, the two Gonlan represent Pyrthera's two elemental registers: the fire that falls from the sky and the fire that rises from the ground. Both are in mourning at the start of the series — children of a murdered god, feeling the sickness from Hel spread through worlds their father built.
