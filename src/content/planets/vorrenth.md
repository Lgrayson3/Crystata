Vorrenth's twin suns bleach the landscape into endless dunes of pale silica. Beneath the sand lie the Hollows — a sprawling network of precursor vaults sealed by mechanisms no living species can decipher. The nomadic clans that traverse the surface fight endlessly over access to the Hollow entrances, believing the ancient technology within can reshape reality itself.
