Aethermoor sits at the gravitational center of the known cosmos, its crystalline crust refracting starlight into prismatic auroras that arc across the sky at all hours. The planet's unique geological composition — veins of resonant crystal threaded through basalt and obsidian — makes it a natural amplifier for arcane energies.

Civilizations have risen and fallen here for millennia, each drawn by the promise of the Crystata — the planetary lattice of interconnected crystal nodes said to hold the memory of every spell ever cast on its surface.
